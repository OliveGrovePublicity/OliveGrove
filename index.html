Olive Grove Publicity helps authors connect with readers. Our offerings include strategy, creative, & over five years publicity experience with small press and academic audiences. Reach out about your forthcoming book and we'll help you find your focus.

olivegrovepublicity@gmail.com

Olive Grove Publicity is a project of Zoe S Donald, independent publicist, graphic designer, and the managing editor of the small press Diode Editions.

The Olive Grove Publicity logo features an abstracted olive leaf, inspired by the Palestinian kufiya. As such, we remind ourselves of our pledge to adhere to Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions principles in our practices. To learn more about a free Palestine, visit bdsmovement.net.
